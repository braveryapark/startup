About Reflex Tester

  • Reflex Tester quanitfies reflex response.
  • Created by Avery Park
  • BYU Computer Science
  • In connection with CS 260 Web Programming.
  • Debut March 10th 2023

How to Play

  • Click "Start" to begin your test.
  • Select the mismatching button as fast as you can.
  • If you click an incorrect button, you fail.
  • Click "Stop" to end your test.
  • Your average reflex response time will be displayed.
